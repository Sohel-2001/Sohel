How I Work?

Web Development

I create modern, responsive websites using both coding tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Next.js, and no-code platforms like Webflow, Shopify, and WordPress. From hosting and deployment to SEO optimization, I ensure your website performs flawlessly. Let’s build a site that’s fast, functional, and tailored to your needs.

Graphic Design

Using tools like Canva, Illustrator, Photoshop, and more, I design eye-catching visuals that elevate your brand. Whether it’s social media posts, marketing materials, or unique illustrations, I deliver designs that captivate. Let’s make your ideas stand out in style.

Video Editing

I edit videos with precision and creativity using CapCut, delivering polished and engaging content. Whether for reels, shorts, or presentations, I ensure your videos grab attention and tell your story effectively. Let’s bring your vision to life.

UI/UX Designing

I craft sleek, user-friendly designs with Figma, focusing on intuitive interfaces and exceptional user experiences. From wireframes to prototypes, I create digital designs that engage and delight. Let’s transform your ideas into impactful designs.

Social Media Management (SMM)

I help boost your online presence with content creation, hashtag research, and post designs tailored to your brand. From strategy to execution, I ensure your social media engages your audience effectively. Let’s take your social media to the next level.
